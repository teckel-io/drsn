Dental Testing Services

Comprehensive Diagnostics — Northampton

Dr Sabina Nasir

Overview

At Delapre Dental Care, we believe that accurate diagnosis is the foundation of excellent dental treatment. Our comprehensive range of diagnostic testing helps us identify dental issues early, prevent complications, and plan the most effective treatment for you.

We use the latest dental testing technology and techniques to ensure every patient receives a thorough, precise examination. Whether you're here for a routine check-up or have specific concerns, our diagnostic services are designed to give you complete confidence in your dental health.

Testing Types

Digital X-Rays — Low-radiation imaging that reveals issues hidden beneath the surface, including decay between teeth, bone loss, and impacted teeth. Digital technology allows us to enhance and zoom images for greater precision.

Intraoral Cameras — Tiny cameras that capture detailed views inside your mouth, allowing Dr Nasir to show you exactly what she sees and explain your dental condition clearly.

Periodontal Probing — Precise measurement of gum pocket depths to detect early signs of gum disease and monitor the health of your supporting tissues.

Oral Cancer Screening — A careful visual and tactile examination of your mouth, lips, tongue, and throat to identify any suspicious lesions or abnormalities.

Bite Assessment — Evaluation of how your upper and lower teeth meet, detecting misalignment or bite problems that may cause discomfort or accelerate wear.

Plaque and Tartar Analysis — Assessment of buildup on your teeth to guide cleaning and help you improve your home care routine.

All tests are performed with your comfort and safety as our top priority.

Benefits

Early Detection — Many dental problems begin silently beneath the surface. Our testing catches issues before they become painful or expensive to treat.

Personalised Treatment Planning — Accurate diagnostics allow us to develop a treatment plan tailored specifically to your needs, avoiding unnecessary procedures and focusing on what matters most to your oral health.

Peace of Mind — Know exactly where you stand with your dental health. Regular comprehensive testing provides reassurance and confidence.

Prevention-Focused Care — By identifying risk factors early, we can help you prevent future problems rather than simply reacting to issues after they occur.

Treatment Precision — Detailed diagnostic information ensures Dr Nasir can perform treatment with maximum accuracy and predictability, reducing complications and improving results.

Transparent Communication — We use images and test results to explain your condition clearly, so you understand exactly why we recommend specific treatments.
