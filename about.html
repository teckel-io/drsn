Our Story

For nearly seven decades, Delapre Dental Care has been caring for Northampton families, providing gentle, professional, and patient-centred oral healthcare to our local community.

Founded on the principles of compassion, clinical excellence, and respect for every individual who walks through our door, we believe that exceptional dentistry begins with listening to our patients' concerns, understanding their goals, and delivering care tailored to their unique needs.

Whether you're seeking routine preventive care, restorative treatment, or post graduate guidance, we are here to support your oral health journey with the highest standards of clinical skill and personal attention.

Our Values

Gentle Care — We understand that many patients feel anxious about dental visits. We prioritise your comfort and take time to explain every step of your treatment in clear, reassuring language.

Clinical Excellence — Continuous professional development and evidence-based practice ensure that you receive the most current, effective dental care available.

Patient Partnership — Your voice matters. We work collaboratively with you to develop treatment plans that respect your preferences, budget, and lifestyle.

Accessibility — We provide both NHS and private treatment options, because everyone deserves access to quality dental care regardless of their circumstances.

Professional Integrity — We operate transparently, maintain strict confidentiality, and uphold the highest ethical standards set by the General Dental Council.

Dr Sabina Nasir — Principal Dentist

Dr Sabina Nasir is an accomplished dentist with extensive training in both general dentistry and advanced restorative procedures. She holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and has earned post graduate qualifications in clinical dentistry from the Eastman Dental Institute at University College London.

Her postgraduate education includes a Masters in Fixed and Removable Prosthodontics from King's College London and a postgraduate certificate in implant dentistry, equipping her with the knowledge and technical skills to manage complex cases with precision and confidence.

Registered with the General Dental Council (GDC No. 169400), Dr Nasir is committed to maintaining her professional knowledge through continuous learning and adheres to the GDC's Standards for the Dental Team. Her clinical approach combines technical expertise with genuine empathy for patients' concerns, particularly those who are nervous or have had previous negative experiences with dental care.

When not practising dentistry, Dr Nasir is passionate about supporting dental education and has contributed to the training of future dental professionals.

A Safe Environment for All

We pride ourselves on the quality of care we provide and ask that our patients treat our team with the same respect they receive. Following NHS national policy, we do not tolerate aggression or abuse.

We kindly ask that you refrain from:

  • Aggressive, threatening, or violent behaviour
  • Verbal abuse, including shouting or swearing
  • Any form of discrimination or harassment
  • Damage to our facilities or the use of illegal substances on-site

Our Policy: To protect our staff and patients, we reserve the right to terminate the patient-provider relationship and remove individuals from our list if these standards are not met. Formal reports of all incidents are reviewed by Dr Sabina Nasir and recorded in our clinical safety logs.
