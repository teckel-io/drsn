NHS Treatments

We welcome NHS patients. Treatment is provided in three charge bands set by NHS England.

Band 1
£26.80
Examination, diagnosis, and advice. If clinically needed: X-rays, scale and polish, and preventive care.
Band 2
£73.50
Everything in Band 1, plus: fillings, root canal treatment, and tooth extractions.
Band 3
£319.10
Everything in Bands 1 and 2, plus: crowns, dentures, and bridges.

NHS charges are set by NHS England and subject to change. Some patients are entitled to free NHS dental treatment — ask at reception for details.
