About Oral Surgery

Oral surgery encompasses a range of surgical procedures performed within the mouth and jaw. At Delapre Dental Care, Dr Sabina Nasir provides expert surgical dental care with a focus on patient comfort and safety.

Whether you require a straightforward extraction or a more complex surgical procedure, you can be assured of receiving gentle, professional treatment in a calm and supportive environment.

Procedures We Offer

Our oral surgery services include:

  • Simple and surgical tooth extractions
  • Wisdom tooth removal
  • Complex extractions requiring specialist techniques
  • Oral surgery consultations and assessments
  • Pre-operative and post-operative care

Each procedure is tailored to your individual needs, and Dr Nasir will discuss all options with you before treatment begins.

Your Comfort and Safety

We understand that oral surgery can feel daunting. Dr Sabina Nasir is renowned for her gentle approach and her ability to put anxious patients at ease.

Before any procedure, you will receive a thorough consultation where all aspects of your treatment will be explained clearly. We use modern techniques and local anaesthesia to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.

Post-operative care instructions will be provided to support your recovery, and we are always available to answer any questions or concerns you may have.
