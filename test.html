Dental Tests & Diagnostics

Understanding Your Oral Health Assessment

Dr Sabina Nasir

Why Dental Tests Matter

At Delapre Dental Care, we believe that thorough diagnostic assessment is the foundation of excellent dental care. Our comprehensive testing approach allows Dr Sabina Nasir to detect issues early, plan appropriate treatment, and monitor your oral health over time.

Every patient's mouth is unique. Rather than taking a one-size-fits-all approach, we use a range of diagnostic tools and tests to understand your individual needs, answer your questions, and develop a personalised treatment plan tailored to your goals.

Our Diagnostic Tests
Clinical Examination
Every patient receives a comprehensive visual and tactile examination. Dr Nasir carefully inspects your teeth, gums, jaw, and oral tissues, using special instruments to assess the health of your mouth. This examination forms the foundation of our diagnostic process and allows Dr Nasir to identify cavities, gum disease, wear, and other conditions.
  • Early detection of decay and gum disease
  • Assessment of tooth wear and jaw alignment
  • Screening for oral health concerns
Radiographic Imaging (X-Rays)
Digital X-rays allow Dr Nasir to see areas of your mouth that are not visible during a visual examination, such as decay between teeth, bone loss beneath the gum line, and the roots of your teeth. We use modern digital radiography, which emits significantly lower levels of radiation than traditional film X-rays, ensuring safety whilst providing exceptional diagnostic detail.
  • Detection of hidden decay between teeth
  • Assessment of bone levels and gum health
  • Planning for implants or other treatments
  • Low-radiation digital technology
Intraoral Photography
High-resolution photographs of the inside of your mouth help Dr Nasir document the current state of your teeth and gums, allowing comparison over time and clearer communication about proposed treatments. Photographs are particularly useful for visualising decay, staining, and the alignment of your teeth, and they help us explain treatment recommendations in language you can easily understand.
  • Visual documentation of your oral health
  • Before-and-after records for cosmetic treatments
  • Better communication about treatment options
Periodontal Assessment
Gum health is essential to overall oral health. Dr Nasir measures the pocket depths around each tooth using a calibrated probe, assessing whether your gums are healthy or showing signs of disease. This probing also detects bleeding, recession, and other indicators of periodontal (gum) disease. Early detection allows us to recommend preventive measures before serious problems develop.
  • Detection of gum disease at early stages
  • Baseline measurements for ongoing monitoring
  • Guidance for improved home care
Bite Assessment
The way your upper and lower teeth come together when you bite affects your oral health, comfort, and long-term wear patterns. Dr Nasir assesses your bite alignment, checking for issues like overbite, underbite, or crossbite. Problems with your bite can cause wear on your teeth, jaw pain, and gum disease, making early detection and correction important.
  • Detection of bite problems and misalignment
  • Assessment of risk for tooth wear or jaw pain
  • Planning for orthodontics or other corrections
Our Diagnostic Philosophy

We do not simply check for problems — we help you understand your mouth, prevent future issues, and make informed decisions about your care. Dr Nasir believes that good diagnostics allow for earlier intervention, better outcomes, and ultimately healthier teeth and gums for life.

Every test we perform is explained carefully, and we are always happy to answer your questions. You have the right to understand what we are recommending, why we are recommending it, and what the alternatives are.
