1. ABOUT THIS PRACTICE

Delapre Dental Care ("the Practice") is operated by Dr Sabina Nasir, a dentist registered with the General Dental Council (GDC) under registration number 169400. We provide both NHS and private dental treatment from our premises at 5 Billing Road, Northampton, NN1 5AN. The Practice is regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC) and operates in accordance with the GDC's Standards for the Dental Team.

2. NHS TREATMENT

NHS dental treatment is provided in accordance with the National Health Service (Dental Charges) Regulations 2005 and current NHS contract terms. NHS charges are set by the Department of Health and Social Care and organised into charge bands (Band 1, 2, or 3). You will be advised of the applicable band before treatment begins. Certain patients are entitled to free NHS treatment, including children under 18, pregnant women, and those who have recently given birth. If you believe you are eligible for free treatment, please inform us at your appointment. It is your responsibility to provide accurate information regarding your exemption status.

3. PRIVATE TREATMENT

Private dental treatment is offered at fees agreed in writing prior to commencement. A written treatment plan and cost estimate will be provided before any chargeable private treatment begins. Payment terms will be confirmed at consultation. The Practice may require a deposit for laboratory work or specialist appointments. Estimates are provided in good faith, though final costs may vary if clinical circumstances change during treatment; any such variation will be discussed with you before proceeding.

4. APPOINTMENTS

Appointments may be booked by telephone during normal office hours (Monday–Friday, 8:15am to 5pm). We ask that you arrive on time for your appointment. The Practice reserves the right to reschedule if you arrive more than ten minutes late. If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please provide at least 24 hours' notice. Repeated failure to attend or to give adequate notice may result in your removal from the Practice's patient list. A cancellation charge may be applied for private appointments where less than 24 hours' notice is given.

5. CLINICAL CARE & PROFESSIONAL STANDARDS

All clinical decisions are made by qualified dental professionals registered with the GDC, in accordance with their professional duty of care and the GDC's Standards for the Dental Team. Treatment recommendations are based on clinical examination and your individual oral health needs. Whilst we aim for the best possible outcomes, no guarantee of any specific clinical result can be given; dentistry involves inherent clinical risks which will be discussed with you as part of the informed consent process. You have the right to accept or decline recommended treatment. Declining recommended treatment may affect your oral health.

6. COMPLAINTS & FEEDBACK

We welcome feedback and take complaints seriously. If you are dissatisfied with your treatment or care, please contact us directly. We operate a formal complaints procedure in line with NHS and CQC requirements and will respond to complaints within 10 working days. If you remain dissatisfied, you may refer your complaint to the Dental Complaints Service (private treatment), NHS England (NHS treatment), or the General Dental Council.

7. CONSENT & RECORDS

By attending the Practice, you consent to examination and treatment as discussed with your dentist. We keep confidential clinical records of all treatment. You have the right to access your records and to request copies. Records are retained in accordance with professional guidance and legal requirements, typically for 10 years from your last appointment.
