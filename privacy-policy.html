Privacy Policy

Your Data Protection Rights

Delapre Dental Care

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Delapre Dental Care, we take your privacy and the security of your personal information very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

We are committed to being transparent about what information we hold and how we use it. If you have any questions about this policy or your personal data, please contact us directly.

What Information We Collect & Why

Identity and Contact Information: Your full name, date of birth, address, telephone number, email address, and NHS number (if applicable).

Dental and Medical Records: Details of your dental and general medical history, current medications, allergies, treatment records, clinical examination findings, radiographs (X-rays), and clinical photographs.

Payment Information: Records related to NHS dental charges, exemption status declarations, and private treatment financial agreements.

Communication Records: Correspondence with the practice, appointment confirmations, recall notices, and complaint letters.

We collect this information to provide you with safe, effective dental care and to fulfil our legal and regulatory obligations under NHS law, GDC (General Dental Council) standards, and CQC (Care Quality Commission) regulations.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you. We will respond within one calendar month.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your information in specific situations.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing where we rely on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us in writing at: Delapre Dental Care, 5 Billing Road, Northampton, NN1 5AN.

How We Protect Your Information

Your personal information is held securely both in paper form (in locked, secure filing cabinets) and electronically (in encrypted, password-protected practice management systems). Only authorised members of staff have access to your records.

We share your information only when necessary and only with organisations involved in your care or required by law, including your GP, dental specialists, NHS England, and regulatory bodies. We never sell or pass your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We retain your dental records for a minimum of 10 years following your last appointment, in accordance with NHS guidance and professional standards. After this period, records are securely destroyed.
