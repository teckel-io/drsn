Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Delapre Dental Care

Appointments & Booking
How do I book an appointment?
To book an appointment, please call us on 01604 636836 during office hours (Monday–Friday, 8:15am to 5pm). All patients are seen by appointment only. We will do our best to accommodate your preferred time.
What are your office hours?
We are open Monday to Friday from 8:15am to 5pm. We close for lunch from 12:30pm to 1:30pm. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.
Can I book an appointment online?
Currently, we accept appointments by telephone only. Please call us on 01604 636836 to book your appointment. Our team will be happy to find a time that suits you.
What should I do if I have a dental emergency?
If you have a dental emergency, please contact the practice on 01604 636836 as soon as possible. We will do our best to see you urgently. If you are unable to reach us outside office hours, please contact NHS 111 or your local emergency dental service.
Do I need to bring anything to my appointment?
Please bring a form of identification and your NHS number if you are an NHS patient. If you are a new patient, please arrive 10 minutes early to complete registration forms. If you have dental records from another practice, please ask them to be sent to us in advance.
NHS & Private Dentistry
Do you accept NHS patients?
Yes, we accept both NHS and private patients. Dr Sabina Nasir and her team provide professional dental care under both the NHS and private payment schemes. Please ask about availability when you call to book.
What is the difference between NHS and private dentistry?
NHS dentistry is funded by the National Health Service and provides essential dental care at a subsidised cost. Private dentistry is paid for directly by the patient and may offer additional treatments or shorter waiting times. Both NHS and private patients receive high-quality care from Dr Nasir.
How much does NHS treatment cost?
NHS dental treatment is provided at set charges determined by the NHS. Costs vary depending on the type of treatment you need. Please call us on 01604 636836 to discuss costs for your specific treatment needs.
Can I switch from NHS to private treatment?
Yes, you can switch between NHS and private treatment. Please discuss your preferences with Dr Nasir or our reception team when you book your appointment or during your visit.
Are you currently accepting new NHS patients?
Please call us on 01604 636836 to enquire about NHS patient availability. Our team will be able to advise you on current capacity and help you register if we are accepting new patients.
Patient Care & Treatment
What should I expect during my first visit?
During your first visit, we will take a full medical and dental history, examine your teeth and gums, and discuss any concerns you may have. We may take X-rays to assess your dental health. Dr Nasir will explain her findings and discuss any treatment options with you.
How often should I have a dental check-up?
Most patients should have a dental check-up every six months. However, the frequency may vary depending on your individual dental health. Dr Nasir will advise you on the best schedule for your needs during your appointment.
Do you offer teeth cleaning and hygiene appointments?
Yes, we offer professional teeth cleaning and hygiene appointments. Regular cleaning helps prevent gum disease and keeps your teeth healthy. Please ask about hygiene appointments when you call to book.
What should I do if I am nervous about dental treatment?
Dr Nasir and her team are experienced in treating nervous patients. Please let us know about your concerns when you book your appointment. We will take time to explain procedures, answer your questions, and ensure you feel comfortable throughout your treatment.
How can I maintain good oral health between appointments?
Brush your teeth twice daily with fluoride toothpaste, floss daily, and limit sugary foods and drinks. Regular dental check-ups and professional cleaning are also important. Dr Nasir and her team will provide personalised advice on oral hygiene during your visits.
