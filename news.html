About Our Practice

Delapre Dental Care, led by Dr Sabina Nasir, provides compassionate, professional dental care to patients across Northampton. With advanced qualifications in fixed and removable prosthodontics, implant dentistry, and clinical dentistry, Dr Nasir is committed to delivering the highest standards of evidence-based care.

Whether you require NHS dental treatment or private services, we welcome you to visit us at 5 Billing Road. Our practice is regulated by the Care Quality Commission (CQC) and meets all relevant professional and regulatory standards set by the General Dental Council (GDC).

To make an appointment or discuss your dental health needs, please call us on 01604 636836 or email office@delepredental.com.
